Meet Our Doctor

Dr. Omar Ben Haj Said is a highly skilled and experienced ophthalmologist who is dedicated to providing the highest level of care to his patients. He received his Intercollegiate Diploma in Ocular Surface Diseases from the University of Tours in France and is a member of the European Society of Cataract and Refractive Surgery (ESCRS) and the American Academy of Ophthalmology (AAO).

Dr. Omar is particularly skilled in the treatment of cataracts, glaucoma, and other complex eye conditions. He has also undergone specialized training in refractive surgery, including a fellowship in Park Avenue LASEK NYC, (which is the most advanced method for laser vision correction. This method is 100% non-cutting and 100% non-invasive, making it 10 times safer than LASIK).

Aside from his clinical work, Dr. Omar is also the CEO of INAYA MEDICAL, an innovative medical practice that provides a comprehensive range of eye care services. Whether you need a routine eye exam or advanced treatment for a complex eye condition, you can trust Dr. Omar to provide expert, compassionate care that is tailored to your individual needs.
